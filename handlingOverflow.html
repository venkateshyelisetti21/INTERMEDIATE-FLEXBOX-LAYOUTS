Virat Kohli is an iconic Indian cricketer, renowned for his aggressive batting and leadership. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he rose through the ranks to become one of the world's best batsmen. Kohli has set numerous records across all formats, including being the fastest to 10,000 ODI runs. As captain, he led India to numerous victories, known for his intense dedication and fitness. Off the field, Kohli is also a fitness enthusiast and a philanthropist, with a significant influence on and off the cricket pitch. His passion and drive make him a modern cricket legend.
